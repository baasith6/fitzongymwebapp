Workout Tips for Beginners

Published on: April 26, 2025

Starting your fitness journey can feel overwhelming, but small steps lead to big results. Begin with a clear goal, whether it's weight loss, strength building, or flexibility improvement.

Stick to simple routines initially — think squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. Gradually increase intensity as your body adapts. Remember, consistency beats intensity in the beginning.

Stay hydrated, get enough sleep, and prioritize recovery days to avoid burnout. Always listen to your body. Fitness is a journey, not a race.
